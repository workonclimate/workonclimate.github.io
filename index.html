About us

Eugene and Cass were two software engineers at Google who cared about climate but were unsure about how to make a difference. By connecting with each other and taking action, they gained the confidence to leave their jobs. Eugene's goodbye email went viral on LinkedIn reaching 500K views, bootstrapping the core team and community we have today.

We deeply believe climate change is the most important problem facing humanity. We have 10-20 years to reach net zero emissions and stay there forever. All of us on the Work on Climate core team are fully invested in helping you find a startup cofounder or impactful job in the climate space. Come and see our stories and share yours.
